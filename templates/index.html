About Us

University life is expensive, but it does not have to be, ReCircle campus is your go-to student marketplace where you can find affordable deals and give your old stuff a new home. We are building a culture of making, saving and supporting each other on Campus.

We also partner with retailers, malls and brands to bring students exclusive discounts and clearance stock, giving them direct access to the student market while promoting a culture of reuse. Together, we are shaping the future of a sustainable student economy.

Overall ReCircle campus aims to create a sustainable student economy, making life easier, more affordable, and environmentally friendly for students.

How does ReCircle campus benefit students

Convenient marketplace

ReCircle provides a campus-based marketplace where students can easily buy and sell items, saving time and effort.

Sustainable Living

By promoting reuse and recycling, ReCircle encourages sustainable living and reduces waste on campus.

Affordability

The platform offers discounts and affordable prices, making it easier for students to access the items they need

Opprtunity to declutter and earn

Students can declutter their spaces and earn money by selling items they no longer need

Acess to brands and retailers

ReCircle connects students with forward-thinking brands and retailer, providing exclusive discounts and promotions

Students safety

At ReCircle Campus, safety comes firs. Every buyer and seller is part of a verified campus community, which helps create a trusted environment.
