Partner With Us

At ReCircle Campus, we're building more than a marketplace. We are creating a sustainable student economy that connects students, brands, and communications in a culture of reuse.

Why partner with us?

Direct Access to students: Connect with over 2 million Nigerian university students through pop-up markets, digital resale groups, and our online platform.

Move stock faster: Clear discounted or seasonal inventory by selling directly to students who are actively looking for affordable essentials.

Boost your brand image: Position your company as student-friendly and sustainability-driven, building long-term loyalty.

Drive community impact: support students in saving money, reducing waste, and living sustainably.

By partnering with ReCircle Campus, you're not just selling products, you are investing in the future of a circular, student-powered economy. Together, we can turn what students have into what they need, while shaping a more affordable and sustainable future.
